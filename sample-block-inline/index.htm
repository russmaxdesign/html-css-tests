CSS: are they inline or inline-block?

Below are a series of elements defined as "inline". However some of them are actually "inline-block".

For an inline element:

  • Width does not apply
  • Height does not apply
  • Overflow does not apply
  • Margin is applied but only affects content to the left and right
  • Padding is applied but only affects content to the left and right
  • Border is applied but only affects content to the left and right
